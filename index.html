How I Run an Account

Twenty years in IT and program management, and it was never the tooling that carried me: not the Gantt charts, not the kanban boards, not the sea of reports. What carried me was the customer. Their definition of success, seen from high enough that the whole picture fits in one frame — that is the work I love. I win when the customer does: their success made legible to both sides, their executives and our engineers, until delivery, renewal, and expansion stop being separate conversations. At SMPL Finance, that meant pulling both into one version of the truth — shared numbers, shared milestones — and the weekly collisions between release, marketing, and compliance stopped.

LISTEN
Learn what they are actually carrying. Not the org-chart version: revenue targets, campaign dates, budget runway.
TRANSLATE
Bridge the two languages. Specs into business outcomes; business pressure into precise, respectful asks.
DELIVER
Make trust compound. Honest status, early escalation, and proof — renewals start from evidence.
